The National Identification Number (NIN) is your unique, lifelong identity assigned by the Federal Government of Nigeria. It is confidential and may only be released for legitimate transactions. You will be notified when your National Identity Card (NIC) is ready for collection at the enrollment centre. For enquiries, contact NIMC using the details below.
